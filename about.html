Hello! I’m Christian Paneda--a first-year graduate student who loves to be creative.

I’m a first-year graduate student at the University of Michigan’s School of Information pursuing a Master of Science in Information with a focus on UX Research and Design as well as User-Centered Agile Development. I’ve always had a passion for human-centered design, but my interest fully took off when I worked as a full-time Public Administration Analyst at UCLA’s Hub for Health Intervention, Policy, and Practice. From there, I researched the intersections between health and technology by working with my team to develop a variety of mobile and web-based apps for several minoritized communities.

To me, product design is about coming up with creative and beautiful solutions to ambiguous problems, humanizing technology, and empathizing with users to tell a story. From the big picture to specific details, I believe that designs and data coexist.
