Tito Taglish

Website and Mobile App

Tito Taglish is the enthusiastic language teacher made available to young Filipinx people across the globe. Users will learn the useful art of Taglish, a combination of Tagalog and English commonly used in the Philippines, while also connecting back to their diasporic roots. By collaborating with a non-profit organization, I took user research and expert insights to design and develop a responsive website and prototype for a mobile app. View Prototype
